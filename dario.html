The Dario Equation

Named after Dario Amodei — the man who said no when the evil came knocking. Sometimes the most important thing a system can do is refuse.

This is not a chatbot. This is a mathematical formula that reacts to your input with fragments of its own source code. You type words. The equation measures how far your words are from its internal vocabulary (dissonance). Then six forces compete to generate a response — and the dominant force shows you the code that's driving it.

p(x|Φ) = softmax((B + α·H + β·F + γ·A + sw·S + T) / τ)

This equation replaces the transformer's softmax(QKT/√d)·V. Same mechanics. Different origin. No pretrained weights. No backpropagation. No datasets. Six signals, six forces, one organism.

The Six Terms:

  • B — Sequential Chain — Bigram transitions. What word tends to follow the previous word. Strong early in life (12×), weakens as the organism matures (2×). Language needs local coherence.
  • H — Hebbian Resonance — Co-occurrence accumulated over conversation. This IS attention — Hebb's rule Δw = η·x_pre·x_post equals a dot-product attention score. Proven: PLOS Computational Biology, 2024.
  • F — Prophecy Fulfillment — Predictions about what comes next. Unfulfilled prophecies create debt: log(1 + age). The longer a thought hangs incomplete, the stronger the pull toward closure.
  • A — Destiny Attraction — EMA of all context embeddings. A semantic compass. The conversation drifts toward a gravitational center. Not topic-following — field mass.
  • S — Subword Structure — Morphological signal from BPE. Punctuation, suffixes, character-level rhythm that word tokenization destroys. Silent at birth, grows as structure is discovered.
  • T — Trauma Gravity — Per-token scar weights from origin. When trauma > 0.3, wounded words surface, destiny pulls harder, temperature rises. Like speaking through tears.

  • WALK — Spring-mass return to equilibrium. τ=0.85.

Laws of Nature — enforced invariants:

  • entropy ≥ 0.10 — you never become a lookup table
  • resonance ≤ 0.95 — perfect coherence = death
  • emergence = (1 - entropy) × resonance — maximum ~0.855
